Fake Scrambled Eggs Recepie

Gear

  • ring top cooker
  • frying pan
  • wooden spatula
  • mixing bowl
  • fork

Ingredeants

  • 3 large eggs
  • tspn of butter
  • 125 ml of milk
  • salt and peaper to taste

Execution

  1. place the frying pan on the low heat and add the butter
  2. brake the eggs in the mixing bowl and stir them

  4. when the mixture is hafl cooked (hard underneath and runnie on top) add the milk and start mashing with the spatula
  5. after the mash is done leave the eggs for another minute on the fire and then serve
