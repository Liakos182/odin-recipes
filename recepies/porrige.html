Porrige Recepie

Gear

  • microwave oven
  • tea spoon
  • non metalic bowl

Ingridiants

  • 40 gr of Roaled Oats
  • 250ml of full fat milk
  • tsp of honey
  • optionalnuts and berries

Execution

  1. place the milk and oats in a bowl, proboly let them sit for a couple of minutes
  2. microwave the bowl for a minute
  3. remove the bowl from the microwave and melt in the teaspoon of honney till there is no honey on the spoon
  4. return the bowl to the mickrove for 40 seconds
  5. remove the bowl from the microwave and stir again till all content is homogenus
  6. add nuts and berries to preference
