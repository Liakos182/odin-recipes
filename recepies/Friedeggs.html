Fried Eggs Recepie

Gear

  • ring cooker
  • frying pan
  • matching lid for frying pan
  • table spoon

Ingridiants

  • 2 large eggs
  • tbs of olive oil
  • tbs of tap water
  • pinch of salt
  • pinch of black peaper
  1. Put the pan empty on low heat.
  2. After a minute add the tbsp of olive oil,wait for another minute.
  3. Crack eggs and drop them in to the pan,make sure dosen't brake and that there is no shell in the eggs.
  4. Rait for a couple more minutes, add salt and peaper.
  5. Drop a the tbsp of water on the metal part of the pan, not on the eggs put the lid on the pan right away.
  6. Leave the pan on the heat for two more minutes, then remove pan from heat and leave for another minute.
  7. Remove the lid from the pan and serve eggs plain or on toasted breads.
